Destinations

Paris

Paris

It may be a cliché but Paris really does epitomise style. Small boho B&B’s are considered up-and-coming and high-end luxury hotels are celebrated for their flamboyancy. 3* Paris Eiffel Cambronne is one of those offering typical Parisian charm and turns out to be one of our most popular. 4* Golden Tulip Opera De Noailles is located into the chic Opera district, the perfect spot for enjoying some retail therapy. While 5* Four Seasons George V lives up to its name and its prestigious setting near the Champs Elysees.

See our carefully selected range of hotels in Paris

London

London

Experience London in all its glory when you stay at any of our London hotels. All of our hotels are chosen for their locations and they range from traditional well-known names to fabulous boutique hotels in the Mr & Mrs Smith collection . The 3* President has an enviable location within walking distance of so many of London's main sights. 3* Kensington Close , just off High Street Kensington, is a great location for shopping, while 4* Rembrandt , opposite the V&A, is perfectly placed for exploring London’s fascinating museums. 4* Aloft lets you see a different side of London and offers incredible value for money. The luxury 5* Athenaeum has to be one of our favourites, with its attention to detail extending to the complimentary soft drinks and snacks supplied in your in-room minibar. The award winning afternoon tea and whiskey bar is equally impressive.

For a wider choice of hotels, see our London travel guide

Rome

Rome

Steeped in history and tradition, all of our hotels in Rome offer an authentic and cultural encounter for you to experience. The family-run 3* Regno has a great location in the historic centre close to the amazing Santa Maria della Rotonda, or choose our top selling hotel 4* Dei Mellini and enjoy an aperitif on the rooftop terrace during the summer months. For a touch of luxury the 5* Regina Hotel Baglioni enjoys a great location on Via Veneto, the 5* Gran Meliá Rome is also pretty special with its breathtaking 360° views of the Eternal City, but it’s actually the intimate terrace gardens at the 5* Hotel De Russie that may surprise you the most.

Experience the local flavour and spirit of Rome with our travel guides

Barcelona

Barcelona

All of our hotels in Barcelona have been chosen for being the very best of their kind, from traditional large hotels to smaller, boutique properties. What they have in common is that each offers something unique. If you’re looking for an authentic Spanish feel the 3* Meson Castilla is a great choice, in a terrific, affordable location. The 4* H10 Marina is one of our best sellers and delivers a high standard for your money. For all out luxury head to the 5* Mandarin Oriental to experience its incredible spa and Michelin-starred dining.

Discover more with our Barcelona hotels travel guide

Dublin

Dublin

Dublin’s easy going atmosphere makes it such an appealing city to visit. The 3* Best Western Plus Academy Plaza prides itself on its culinary delights with its award-winning Asian restaurant. The 4* Ashling Hotel has a fantastic reputation for providing a high-level of service and is one of our best sellers, while 4* The Morrison Hotel is fast becoming the hippest and coolest hotel in Dublin city. For five star luxury The Shelbourne Dublin A Renaissance Hotel is a national treasure.

See more of our recommended hotels in Dublin

Glasgow

Glasgow

You’ll soon discover why Glasgow is often referred to as the UK’s style capital and is fast becoming the best shopping destination outside of London. Stay at our top selling cheap hotel, 3* Holiday Inn Express Glasgow - City Centre Riverside , close to St Enoch's Shopping centre and a short bus ride down to SECC – the new Hydro Arena showing the latest concerts. For something a little different, our favourite boutique property, 4* Hotel Indigo Glasgow has a striking history – housed in a power station with a fascinating story to tell. Or enjoy vintage luxury when you stay at the iconic 5* Blythswood Square , which plays homage to the Monte Carlo Rally in 1955 that started from this point.

Find out where to stay in Glasgow

Edinburgh

Edinburgh

From the famous cobbled streets of the Royal Mile in the Old Town to the beautiful Georgian facades lining the grand avenues of the New Town, Edinburgh is a city of contrasts. Right at the heart you’ll find 3* Holiday Inn Express Edinburgh , which offers incredible value for money. Or choose our best selling hotel, 4* Hilton Edinburgh Grosvenor Hotel in the West End – perfect for shopping and eating out. 4* King James by Thistle is another favourite, where you’ll experience traditional Scottish hospitality. For something a little special 5* The Caledonian A Waldorf Astoria Hotel won’t disappoint – Martini's Friday at the Caley bar will start the weekend off in style and the fantastic Jazz Sunday lunch will be the finishing touch.

Find the best places to stay in Edinburgh

Manchester

Manchester

With Manchester’s cutting edge vibe permeating its culture and dominating its music scene, you’re never far from all the action. Stay at our top seller, 3* The Portland Thistle - just five minutes’ stroll from the Arndale Centre - the largest city centre shopping mall in Europe, where you’ll discover the latest British trends. If you have tickets to an event at G-Mex, MICC or Manchester Arena, 4* Palace is perfectly located. Or be part of Manchester’s in crowd at 5* Lowry Hotel – proving to be Manchester's most fashionable stay, bursting with energy, on the banks of the river in the heart of the city.

Find more recommended Manchester hotels
