Blaze a trail

If you worked for Starfleet, you could get away with wearing your Star Trek uniform to work every day. But you don't. So you have to wear whatever's part of the dress code policy at your office. And for a lot of folks that looks suspiciously like a suit. Not even a spacesuit. Just a suit. Dangit.

Amp up your next interview with this Star Trek TOS Open-Front Blazer. Part of the ThinkGeek Star Trek Collection by Her Universe, it's available exclusively at ThinkGeek. It features a notched lapel, jetted functional pockets, and princess seams in the front. The lining print is TOS insignia, and it peeks out if you roll the cuffs. The piping is red, as a nod to Uhura. We're hoping is more like a red power tie and less akin to a red landing party member. But, you know, we'll keep an eye on that. Let us know in the comments below if your red blazer gets you the pink slip. On the plus side, we have lots of Star Trek goodies to keep you entertained while you're unemployed should it come to that....
